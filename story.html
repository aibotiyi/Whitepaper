The Genesis: From Research to Reality

Why We Started

AIBOT IYI Protocol didn't begin as a typical blockchain project. Our journey started years ago as a specialized AI research company focused on one critical mission: building intelligent systems to support cryptocurrency trading.

While the crypto markets operated 24/7 with incredible volatility, most individual investors lacked access to the sophisticated algorithms and computational power that institutional traders used to generate consistent profits. We saw this gap and decided to bridge it.

Building the Foundation

Over years of dedicated research and development, we built our technology stack from the ground up. Our approach was methodical and focused on two fundamental pillars that would become the backbone of our ecosystem:

Massive GPU Infrastructure

We assembled a fleet of 2,000+ high-performance GPUs distributed across strategic global data centers, creating unparalleled computational power for AI model training and real-time market analysis.

Proprietary AI Algorithms

Through continuous refinement and machine learning, we developed proprietary trading algorithms that learn from market patterns, adapt to volatility, and execute trades with precision that surpasses human capability.

Global Infrastructure: Powering the Future

Our commitment to excellence led us to establish data centers across 12 strategic locations worldwide, ensuring optimal performance, redundancy, and 24/7 operations across all time zones.

The Evolution: From AI Company to DeFi Pioneer

2019-2021

Foundation Phase

Established core AI research team and began developing proprietary machine learning algorithms for cryptocurrency market analysis. Initial GPU infrastructure deployment.

2022

Scale & Expansion

Expanded global data center network to 12 locations. Reached 2,000+ GPU milestone. Achieved consistent 87.3% win rate in live trading environments.

2023

DeFi Vision

Recognized the potential to democratize our technology through blockchain. Began developing AIBOT IYI Protocol to make advanced AI trading accessible to everyone.

2024

Protocol Launch

Launch of comprehensive ecosystem combining AI trading, GPU mining, and DeFi protocols. Opening access to institutional-grade tools for retail investors globally.

Our Mission Today

Today, AIBOT IYI Protocol represents the culmination of years of research, development, and real-world testing. We've transformed from a specialized AI company into a comprehensive DeFi ecosystem that democratizes access to sophisticated financial technology.
