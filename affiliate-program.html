Advanced Compensation System

Binary compression plan with same-rank appreciation for two adjacent generations

Team Bonus Compression

Team bonuses are compressed when there are equal or higher levels in your downline.

Rule: Your effective bonus = Your level% - Highest downline level%
• Level 1 (1%) in downline → You get compressed by 1%
• Level 2 (2%) in downline → You get compressed by 2%
• Level 3 (3%) in downline → You get compressed by 3%

Same-Rank Appreciation

When you have same-level partners in your upline, they earn from your team bonus.

Only 2 adjacent generations: F1 and F2 upline from you
• F1 Same Level: Gets 8% of your team bonus
• F2 Same Level: Gets 5% of your team bonus
• Lower Level: Gets 0% (skipped to next)
